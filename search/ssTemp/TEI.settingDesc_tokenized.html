<settingDesc>

(setting description) describes the setting or settings within which a language interaction takes place, or other places otherwise referred to in a text, edition, or metadata. [15.2. Contextual Information 2.4. The Profile Description]
corpus
Attributes att.global (xml:id, n, xml:lang) (lim.att.global.fragmentable (part)) (att.global.rendition (rendition)) (att.global.analytic (ana)) (att.global.source (source))
lim: limItem
core: note
corpus: setting
May contain a prose description organized as paragraphs, or a series of setting elements. If used to record not settings of language interactions, but other places mentioned in the text, then place optionally grouped by listPlace inside standOff should be preferred.

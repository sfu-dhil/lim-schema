teidata.duration.iso

 defines the range of attribute values available for representation of a duration in time using ISO 8601 standard formats
tei
A duration is expressed as a sequence of number-letter pairs, preceded by the letter P; the letter gives the unit and may be Y (year), M (month), D (day), H (hour), M (minute), or S (second), in that order. The numbers are all unsigned integers, except for the last, which may have a decimal component (using either

T

For complete details, see ISO 8601 Data elements and interchange formats — Information interchange — Representation of dates and times.
